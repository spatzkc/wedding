Dining

While you are in central Arkansas, we have some recommendations for ways to pass the time (between celebrations of course)! Emily’s favorite coffee shop in Conway is Blue Sail Coffee located downtown. In Little Rock, Emily and Kevin both enjoy River City Coffee in the Heights neighborhood. Our favorite restaurants in Little Rock include The Fold: Botanas & Bar and Tokyo House sushi buffet. In Conway, we enjoy Umami hibachi grill and sushi house and Layla’s Gyros and Pizzeria. For a nice dinner or lunch, we recommend Mike’s Place in Conway and The Pantry in Little Rock.

Attractions

If you are itching to get outdoors, we enjoy the views on top of Pinnacle Mountain in Little Rock and the hike down to the waterfalls at Petit Jean Mountain in Conway County. Woolly Hollow State Park is a lovely swimming and paddle boating spot just outside of Conway. The Big Dam bridge and the Riverwalk are centrally located in Little Rock for a shorter walk with great views of the Arkansas River. For indoor family fun, Altitude Trampoline Park, Museum of Discovery, and exhibits at the William J. Clinton Presidential Library and Museum are great choices!
